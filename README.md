## About me

### Side Hustle - Memorable Experiences

I own an ephemeral cocktails bar for private events.

We do bring joy to your events and craft Memorable Experiences for you and your guests.

I was sick of seeing tech startups spend money in cocktails setup that didn't meet the standard I have in terms of taste and quality of service. So I built one.

We rely on handmade fresh products and chose wisely which bottles we use.

Our game is not about using cheap products to increase margin, it's about providing the best experience so that clients come back.

We do partner with Lumo Place for catering and mocktails (<- alcohol free cocktails).

[Memorable Experiences](https://www.instagram.com/memorable_xp) • [Lumo Place](https://www.instagram.com/lumoplace)
